Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

GrimWar ("we", "the game") is a browser-based multiplayer wizard arena game. This privacy policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Player Names: You provide a display name when joining a game. This is only used during gameplay and is not stored permanently.

Connection Data: The game uses peer-to-peer (WebRTC) connections. Your IP address may be visible to other players in the same game session. We do not log or store IP addresses.

Game Data: Game state (scores, spell selections, cosmetic choices) exists only during active game sessions and is not saved to any server.

Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this website and other websites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

Cookies

This site may use cookies for advertising purposes via Google AdSense. No other cookies are set by the game itself.

Children's Privacy

This game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
